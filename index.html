Holmwell Software

Hello,

Holmwell is a software company in Corvallis, Oregon.

Our mission is to help small organizations capitalize on the collaborative power of the Internet; to help our communities share, notably services, things, and knowledge; to specifically work for the poor.

We make Circle Blvd., a task management site for community projects. It is open to the public and free to use.

We host Code Corvallis, a monthly, 2-hour unconference about software. It is also open to the public and free to attend.

We sponsor the Corvallis Arts Walk. Our Android app is free to download. Coming soon for iPhone.

Our latest news is posted to Twitter, at @_holmwell and @circleblvd.
